## Privacy Policy Template

**Host at:** `https://rwp3app.com/privacy`

### Required Sections:

1. **What information we collect**
   - Personal info: name, email, phone number, gender, profile photo
   - Location data: GPS coordinates during ride booking and tracking
   - Payment info: processed via Stripe (we don't store card numbers)
   - Device info: device model, OS version, app version
   - Usage data: ride history, search queries, app interactions
   - Driver-specific: government ID documents, vehicle details, bank account info
   - Communication: chat messages between rider and driver
   - Emergency contacts (if added by user)

2. **How we use your information**
   - To provide ride-sharing services
   - To match riders with drivers
   - To process payments
   - To send ride status notifications
   - To improve our service (analytics)
   - To ensure safety (SOS alerts, ride tracking)
   - To prevent fraud and abuse

3. **Information sharing**
   - With drivers/riders: name, phone, location (during active ride only)
   - With Stripe: payment processing
   - With Firebase/Google: app infrastructure
   - With law enforcement: if legally required
   - We NEVER sell personal data to third parties

4. **Data retention**
   - Account data: retained while account is active
   - Ride history: retained for 3 years (legal/financial requirement)
   - Financial records: retained for 7 years (tax requirement)
   - Chat messages: retained for 90 days after ride completion
   - Deleted account data: removed within 14 days, financial records anonymized and retained

5. **Your rights**
   - Access your data (in-app profile and history)
   - Correct your data (edit profile)
   - Delete your account (Settings → Delete Account)
   - Export your data (contact support)
   - Opt out of analytics (via App Tracking Transparency on iOS)

6. **Data security**
   - All data encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
   - Data encrypted at rest (Firebase encryption)
   - Payment data handled by PCI-compliant Stripe
   - Driver documents stored in secure Firebase Storage

7. **Children's privacy**
   - Service is for users 18+ only
   - We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18

8. **Location data**
   - Customer: collected only while using the app (booking and tracking)
   - Driver: collected in background only when "online" with active ride
   - Location data is not shared with third parties
   - Users can revoke location permission in device settings

9. **Cookies and tracking**
   - Admin web panel may use cookies for session management
   - Firebase Analytics collects anonymized usage data
   - iOS users: asked for tracking consent via App Tracking Transparency

10. **Contact information**
    - Email: support@rwp3app.com
    - Address: [Your business address]

11. **Changes to this policy**
    - We will notify users of material changes via in-app notification
    - Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance

12. **Effective date**
    - [Date of publication]

8. **Safety**

11. **Dispute resolution**

12. **Termination**
